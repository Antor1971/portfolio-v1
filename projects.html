Club Event Management

Its a university club manangement system website. Members , OCA , Panel Members and Sponsors are four type of users can be handled by this website.The system enables users to register or log in, submit sponsorship applications, view profiles, and receive event notifications. Users can also access detailed event information and, as guests, participate in events. Sponsors can offer financial support, while event panels can request arrangements and publish announcements. The Office of Co-Curricular Activities (OCA) oversees the platform, with authority to approve or reject user registrations, sponsorship and event arrangement requests, and manage event timelines. OCAs can also update user profiles and allocate event funds, ensuring effective coordination and oversight of event-related activities.
